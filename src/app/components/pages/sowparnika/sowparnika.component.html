Background

Sowparnika Homes, a reputed real estate developer headquartered in Bengaluru, is acclaimed for its commitment to delivering high-quality affordable housing. The company has a significant presence in Bengaluru and other key cities in Karnataka, Kerala, and Tamil Nadu.

Challenge

To streamline operations and enhance customer engagement, Sowparnika Homes needed a robust digital solution to manage their daily operations and communication effectively.



Solution

Partnering with Superior Digital, Sowparnika Homes integrated over 70 iPads into their workflow, specifically for Marketing representatives to use in customer presentations, 3D walkthroughs, and Virtual Reality demonstrations. This deployment was managed with a Mobile Device Management (MDM) system that enabled seamless content distribution, policy enforcement, and remote support.

Results

The adoption of digital tools, including the use of iPads for immersive presentations, resulted in improved operational efficiency and enhanced communication across various departments. It allowed Sowparnika Homes to provide better service to customers and adapt quickly to market demands.



Conclusion

Sowparnika Homes successfully leveraged digital technology to enhance their operational capabilities. This collaboration not only improved their workflow efficiency but also reinforced their reputation as a leader in the affordable housing sector in South India.
