Background

Essilor, the world’s largest private optical and ophthalmic research centre, has embarked on a digital transformation journey. With over 170 years of research and more than 430 scientists, Essilor is a leader in optical innovation.

Challenge

Essilor’s Sales and Marketing team required a seamless solution to enhance their product presentations, streamline sharing of product catalogues, and efficiently generate sales orders.



Solution

The deployment of over 400 iPads revolutionised the way Essilor's Sales and Marketing team operates. These devices serve as a vital tool in showcasing product portfolios and facilitating real-time sales order processing.

Results

The integration of iPads has significantly improved the efficiency and effectiveness of Essilor’s sales strategies. By leveraging digital tools, the team can now deliver engaging presentations, quickly access information, and process sales orders on the go.

Conclusion

Essilor’s adoption of iPads highlights the importance of digital tools in modernising sales operations, ultimately enhancing customer engagement and business outcomes.
