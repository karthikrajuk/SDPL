Background

WestPharma is a global leader in the design and production of advanced, high-quality containment and delivery systems for injectable medicines. The company is a trusted partner to leading pharmaceutical and biotechnology firms, committed to improving patient health through innovative solutions.

Challenge

To enhance communication and customer engagement, WestPharma sought to equip its medical representatives and sales teams with advanced mobile technology. The objective was to provide real-time data access and streamline processes to improve sales interactions and productivity.



Solution

As part of its strategy, WestPharma deployed over 200 iPads and 400 iPhones to its sales teams. This deployment included the integration of mobile CRM applications to facilitate the tracking of customer interactions and sales performance. The devices provided easy access to a comprehensive library of resources and product information, enabling more informed discussions with clients.

Results

The introduction of iPads and iPhones led to a 30% increase in sales interactions. The technology enhanced communication, improved workflows, and increased the satisfaction levels of the sales teams. This strategic move has set a new standard for operations in the pharmaceutical industry, underscoring WestPharma's commitment to leveraging technology for superior service delivery.



Conclusion

WestPharma's seamless deployment of mobile technology has successfully positioned the company as a forward-thinking partner in global healthcare solutions. The initiative has reinforced its role as an innovator in pharmaceutical delivery systems, ensuring client satisfaction and improved engagement.
